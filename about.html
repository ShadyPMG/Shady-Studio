About the Artist

I create emotionally driven visual work that explores distortion, warmth, rebellion, and intimacy through form. My practice blends graphic design, photography, illustration, and craft into pieces that question comfort and celebrate emotional honesty.

My work often sits between darkness and vulnerability, using imperfection as a visual language.
